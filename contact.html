Common Questions

How do I report an incorrect shortcut?

Use the form above (select "Bug Report") or email us at bugs@fastshortcuts.com. Please include: which shortcut is wrong, which tool/page it's on, and what the correct shortcut should be. Bonus points for including your OS version!

Can you add shortcuts for [tool name]?

Absolutely! We're always expanding. Send us a request through the form above (select "Tool Request") and tell us which tool you'd like to see. Popular requests get priority.

Do you accept contributions?

Yes! If you want to contribute shortcuts, corrections, or improvements, reach out to us. We're building FastShortcuts for the developer community, and community input is invaluable.

Why haven't you responded to my message?

Check your spam folder first - sometimes our replies end up there. If it's been more than a week, please resend your message. We promise we're not ignoring you; emails occasionally slip through the cracks.

Can I advertise on FastShortcuts?

We don't run ads currently, and we plan to keep it that way. FastShortcuts is built for developers, by developers - no distractions, no sponsored content. Just pure shortcuts.

How can I support FastShortcuts?

The best support is spreading the word! Share FastShortcuts with your dev team, tweet about it, or mention it in your blog. The more people who use it, the more motivated we are to keep improving it.
