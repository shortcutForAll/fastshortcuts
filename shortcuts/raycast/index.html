Raycast Keyboard Shortcuts

Master Raycast—the keyboard-first launcher for Mac and Windows. Every shortcut below is sourced from Raycast's official documentation. Open Raycast with Cmd+Space / Alt+Space, then use the Action Panel (Cmd/Ctrl+K) for every secondary action. Pairs well with our Linear, Notion, and Slack guides.

Why Raycast Shortcuts Matter (July 2026)

Raycast replaces Spotlight, Alfred-style launchers, clipboard managers, and window tools with one keyboard-driven interface. With the Windows public beta expanding the audience beyond Mac, searches for Raycast Windows shortcuts and Raycast hotkeys are rising fast. Memorize three keys first: open Raycast (Cmd/Alt+Space), Action Panel (Cmd/Ctrl+K), and Enter for the primary action. Everything else—favorites, AI Chat, configure command, copy deeplink—lives one Action Panel press away. Assign global hotkeys to your top commands so you never open Root Search for daily actions.

Raycast Shortcuts — Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shortcut to open Raycast?

Default: Cmd+Space on Mac or Alt+Space on Windows. Change it in Settings, and assign per-command global hotkeys from the Action Panel.

What does Cmd+K / Ctrl+K do in Raycast?

It opens the Action Panel for the selected item—secondary actions, copy deeplink, assign hotkey, pin, configure, and more. Type while open to filter actions.

Are Mac and Windows shortcuts the same?

Mostly: Cmd↔Ctrl and Option↔Alt. Exceptions: open Raycast is Cmd+Space vs Alt+Space; Pop to Root is Cmd+Esc vs Shift+Esc.

How do I set a custom hotkey?

Action Panel → Assign Hotkey, or Settings → Shortcuts for a full list. Hotkeys work system-wide even when Raycast is closed.

How do I use AI Chat shortcuts?

Open AI Chat from Root Search (or a hotkey). Use Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+M for model, Cmd/Ctrl+J to send a selected item into chat, and Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+S for the history sidebar.
