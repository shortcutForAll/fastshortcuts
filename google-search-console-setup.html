1 Set Up Google Search Console

Google Search Console is essential for Google to discover and index your site.

Open Google Search Console
  1. Click "URL prefix" (not "Domain")
  2. Enter: https://fastshortcuts.com
  3. Click "Continue"
  4. Choose "HTML tag" verification method
  5. Copy the verification meta tag
  6. Add it to your website's <head> section
  7. Click "Verify"
<meta name="google-site-verification" content="YOUR_CODE_HERE" />
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Add this meta tag to the <head> section of index.html, right after the viewport meta tag.

2 Submit Your Sitemap

Tell Google about all your pages at once.

  1. In Google Search Console, click "Sitemaps" in left menu
  2. Enter: sitemap.xml
  3. Click "Submit"
  4. Wait for "Success" status (may take a few minutes)
‚úÖ Your sitemap URL: https://fastshortcuts.com/sitemap.xml

3 Request Indexing for Key Pages

Manually tell Google to index your most important pages.

  1. In Google Search Console, go to "URL Inspection"
  2. Paste a URL (start with homepage)
  3. Click "Test Live URL"
  4. Click "Request Indexing"
  5. Repeat for 5-10 important pages
Priority Pages to Index:
  • https://fastshortcuts.com
  • https://fastshortcuts.com/shortcuts/vscode/
  • https://fastshortcuts.com/shortcuts/chrome/
  • https://fastshortcuts.com/shortcuts/windows/
  • https://fastshortcuts.com/shortcuts/mac/

4 Build Initial Backlinks

Help Google discover your site faster by getting external links.

Quick Wins (Do Today):

  • Post on Reddit r/webdev or r/programming
  • Submit to Hacker News (Show HN)
  • Share on Twitter/X with relevant hashtags
  • Post on LinkedIn
  • Submit to Product Hunt
  • Write article on Dev.to mentioning your site
  • Add to your GitHub profile README
  • Submit to web directories (AlternativeTo, etc.)
